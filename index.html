Random Collectibles Order

Introduction

Welcome to Random Collectibles Order. The idea of this randomizer was inspired by RCO (Random Cell Order) on the Teamruns website. This randomizer extends the idea further and will not only randomize the order you have to grab the Power Cells in, but it also forces you to grab other collectibles (e.g., Scout Flies) and complete a series of miscellaneous tasks (e.g., connecting mirrors, pressing buttons, beating bosses) in a specific order. As you not only have to complete more objectives but also a larger variety of them this randomizer will have you revisiting and backtracking a lot and it will most likely come with a great deal of pain.

There may or may not be further updates in the future regarding small red eggs...

Guide

Setup

To use this randomizer you obviously need a copy of Jak And Daxter: The Precursor Legacy. If you want to play on PlayStation or on PC using OpenGOAL is yours to decide. If you run on OpenGOAL feel free to combine this randomizer with other mods.

I would also recommend using a LiveSplit layout that only shows you the next objective you have to complete so there aren't any spoilers. You can either make one yourself or download a basic layout here.

How it works

  • Select a seed. You can either input a seed manually or click the small button next to it to generate a random one.
  • Choose your set of rules. You can decide what gets randomized and what tricks and skips should be allowed.
  • Generate your splits. Click the button below to generate a .lss file to load into LiveSplit.
  • Read the hints section. Before starting I would recommend taking a look into the hints section of this guide.
  • Have fun :)

Hints

Before starting to play I would advise you to read this section as it might help you with later struggles.
  • Softlocks should almost never be possible regardless of your set of rules.
  • If not including the Warp Gate Buttons you should immediately press them once available, as you might softlock yourself out of Snowy Mountain.
  • Some of the rules might cause a lot of pain (e.g. Allow FCS will likely have you do it multiple times).
  • All the collectibles are listed on the right. I would recommend taking a look at these before starting.
  • When playing make sure to only do your current task. Everything not required to do so might be an objective for later.
  • I would also recommend to save your game from time to time in case you accidentally collect something and have to reload.
  • After a little bit of testing I would estimate a playtime of 8-10 hours. Altough I didn't include some of the more painful rules.

Troubleshooting

If there are any logical errors during your playtrough I found it feasable to skip that section and do it at a later point in time or ignore it completely.
If you encounter any logical or technical issues please let me know via Discord and send me a copy of your splits including your settings and seed.
Lastly, I am no professional website designer. I hope it doesn't bother you if this site is a bit wonky at times.
